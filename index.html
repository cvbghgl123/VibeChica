Support

If you encounter issues with VibeChica, try the following:

  • Installation Issues: Ensure your iOS device is updated to the latest version and check your internet connection.
  • Payment Problems: Verify your payment method in the App Store and contact Apple Support for billing or refund issues.
  • App Crashes: Restart your device and reinstall the app if necessary.
